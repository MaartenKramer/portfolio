About me

Web Developer & Game Developer

Hi, my name is Maarten. I'm 25 years old and I study Game Development at the HKU in Utrecht. I've always had a strong passion for all things creative. Whether it's games, music, writing, or making websites like this one! I usually spend my free time playing games, listening to music, or DMing my Dungeons and Dragons campaign.

I'm a bit of a jack of all trades, and I like taking that mindset with me while I work. With the games I make I also like to tackle different genres or themes. Whether it's a dark haunted basement or a secret society of trash worshipping rats, if the debugging isn't too annoying, I'm having a good time!

I'm a Unity focused developer, though I do like to work in different engines when given the option.

DUNGEON CRAWL

Unity Retro Action Game

A Unity based PS1 style action game. The game is a proof of concept designed in only 3 days for a school exposition. The goal of the game is to get to the end of the dungeon whilst fighting skeletons and avoiding falling into any pits. Makes use of multiple filters and self-made textures to give the game a unique Playstation 1 aesthetic. Further achieves this through the low poly models also made by myself.

Used Languages: C#

Used Tools: Blender, GIMP, Audacity

Year: 2022
